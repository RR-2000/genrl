update_params(action: int, reward: float) → None¶

Update parmeters for the policy

Updates the regret as the difference between max quality value and that of the action. Updates the alpha value of beta distribution by adding the reward while the beta value is updated by adding 1 - reward. Update the counts the action taken.

  • reward (int) – reward obtained for the step
